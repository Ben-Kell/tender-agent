# Transformational application managed services model

Fujitsu’s Transformational Application Managed Services (TAMS) model provides a full lifecycle framework for managing and evolving application environments.

The model:
- Bridges traditional systems and modern digital services
- Enables continuous transformation while maintaining operational stability

Key capabilities include:
- Proactive and reactive service management
- Continuous improvement and optimisation
- Integration of DevOps and Agile practices
- Automated monitoring and performance management

TAMS supports:
- Ongoing innovation and transformation
- Stable operations for critical systems
- Rapid introduction of new capabilities

This approach enables organisations to evolve their application landscape while maintaining reliability and performance.
