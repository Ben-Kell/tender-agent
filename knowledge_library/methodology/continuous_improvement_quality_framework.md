# Continuous improvement and quality management framework

Fujitsu applies a structured quality management framework aligned to international standards to ensure consistent service delivery and continuous improvement.

This includes compliance with:
- ISO 9001 (Quality Management)
- ISO 14001 (Environmental Management)
- ISO 20000 (IT Service Management)
- ISO 27001 (Information Security)

Continuous improvement is driven through:
- Regular performance reviews
- Root cause analysis
- Preventative maintenance practices
- Metrics-based monitoring

Fujitsu focuses on:
- Delivering value to the customer
- Improving efficiency and service quality
- Reducing recurring issues
- Enhancing operational performance over time

This approach ensures services evolve and improve throughout the contract lifecycle.
