# ISO-aligned transition-in methodology for Defence ICT services

Fujitsu applies an ISO 20000-1:2018 certified Transition-In methodology to ensure controlled, auditable, and assured service transition for Defence ICT services.

The methodology is designed to:
- Maintain service continuity
- Validate current-state knowledge
- Establish governance and accountability
- Mitigate transition risks

Transition activities are executed across People, Process, and Technology domains in parallel.

A structured Transition-In period ensures:
- Validation of service readiness
- Confirmation of documentation and processes
- Assurance of operational capability

The methodology avoids assumptions and requires validation of all service elements prior to commencement.

This approach ensures Defence receives a controlled and fully assured transition into service delivery.
