# Structured transition-in phases and activity model

Fujitsu’s Transition-In methodology is delivered through a structured sequence of phases aligned to Defence requirements.

The phases include:

## Mobilise and Initiate
- Establish governance and controls
- Confirm scope and transition plan

## Discover and Baseline
- Validate current-state services and documentation
- Establish baseline knowledge and service registers

## Validate and Assure
- Confirm service management, security, and continuity arrangements
- Update risk and assurance artefacts

## Readiness and Rehearsal
- Validate tools and processes
- Conduct readiness reviews

## Service Commencement
- Formal acceptance of services
- Transition to steady-state governance

Each phase produces defined outputs and requires Defence review and approval before progression.

This structured approach ensures transition is controlled, auditable, and aligned to Defence expectations.
