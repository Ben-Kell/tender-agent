# Governance risk and compliance cyber capability

Fujitsu provides structured governance, risk and compliance services to ensure organisations meet regulatory obligations and maintain secure operations.

This includes:
- Risk assessments and maturity reviews
- Development of cyber security strategies and roadmaps
- Alignment to local and global standards
- Audit and assurance services
- Vulnerability assessments

Fujitsu partners with executive leadership to embed governance into organisational decision-making and ensure compliance across regulated environments.

This capability is particularly suited to government and Defence environments where compliance, auditability and assurance are critical.
