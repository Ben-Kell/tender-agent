# Case Study: Deployed ICT – Supporting the Warfighter

## Overview
Fujitsu delivers end-to-end ICT services for deployed Defence networks, directly supporting operational environments and the warfighter. This engagement demonstrates Fujitsu’s ability to operate in highly dynamic, secure, and mission-critical conditions.

## Customer
Australian Defence Organisation (ADO)

## Scope of Services
- End-to-end support for deployed ICT networks
- Operation across tactical and operational environments
- Integration with Defence’s broader Strategic Information Environment (SIE)

## Key Capabilities Delivered
- Deployment and sustainment of ICT systems in austere environments
- Secure communications and infrastructure support
- Adaptable service delivery aligned to mission needs
- Support across classified domains including Protected, Secret, and Top Secret

## Outcomes
- Reliable ICT support enabling operational effectiveness in the field
- High availability of mission-critical systems
- Demonstrated ability to scale and adapt to changing operational requirements

## Strategic Value
This engagement highlights Fujitsu’s:
- Ability to operate at the tactical edge
- Deep understanding of Defence operational requirements
- Capability to support future initiatives such as Secret Cloud services

## Why It Matters
Deployed ICT is a cornerstone of Defence capability. Fujitsu’s proven delivery in this space reinforces its position as a trusted partner capable of supporting Defence both in enterprise environments and on operations.
