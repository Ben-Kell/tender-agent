# Case Study: Gateway Operations – Secure Defence Connectivity

## Overview
Fujitsu delivers long-standing Gateway Operations services for Defence, managing critical internal gateway infrastructure that underpins secure communications and data exchange across Defence networks.

## Customer
Australian Defence Organisation (ADO)

## Scope of Services
- Management of internal Defence gateways
- 24x7 operational support
- Integration with Defence ITSM and governance frameworks

## Key Capabilities Delivered
- Continuous monitoring and management of gateway infrastructure
- Secure data flow between networks and domains
- Alignment with Defence security and compliance requirements
- Integration with enterprise service management processes

## Outcomes
- Stable and secure gateway operations across Defence environments
- High levels of service reliability and availability
- Trusted delivery of critical national infrastructure services

## Strategic Value
This engagement demonstrates Fujitsu’s:
- Long-term trusted relationship with Defence
- Capability to manage sensitive and critical infrastructure
- Experience operating within complex, multi-domain environments

## Why It Matters
Gateway Operations are essential to Defence’s digital backbone. Fujitsu’s proven delivery ensures secure, reliable connectivity, enabling Defence to operate effectively across enterprise and operational domains.
