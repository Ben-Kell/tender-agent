# Long-term sustainment of COMSARM hazardous inventory system

Fujitsu has sustained the COMSARM® Explosive Ordnance inventory management system for over 30 years, providing Defence with a trusted system for managing hazardous and regulated materials.

The system:
- Maintains full lifecycle records of sensitive items
- Provides immutable audit trails for all activities
- Supports compliance with safety and regulatory requirements
- Enables complete chain-of-custody tracking

Fujitsu has successfully maintained COMSARM across:
- Technology upgrades
- Infrastructure transitions
- Data migrations

while preserving full data integrity and historical accuracy.

This demonstrates Fujitsu’s ability to manage long-term, safety-critical data systems aligned to Defence requirements.
