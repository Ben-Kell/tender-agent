# Defence enterprise ICT delivery footprint and experience

Fujitsu has a long-standing and extensive delivery footprint across the Australian Defence Organisation, supporting services across all domains and classifications.

Fujitsu has:
- Supported Defence for over 30 years
- Delivered services across Protected, Secret and Top-Secret environments
- Operated across both Enterprise and Deployed environments

Key Defence programs include:
- National Deskside Support (NDS)
- Military Platform Integration
- COMSARM logistics system
- Deployed ICT services
- Protected Cloud services
- Gateway Operations

Fujitsu’s delivery spans thousands of engagements and positions the organisation as a trusted and strategic Defence partner.
