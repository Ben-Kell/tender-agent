# Regulatory compliance and audit-ready system design

Fujitsu designs solutions with embedded regulatory compliance and full auditability to meet strict Defence and government requirements.

Capabilities include:
- Built-in regulatory rule enforcement
- Full audit trails and traceability
- Lifecycle data integrity and record management
- Alignment with national and international standards

Systems are designed to:
- Support compliance with regulatory frameworks
- Enable audit readiness at any point in time
- Maintain long-term data integrity

This ensures Defence can meet safety, security and compliance obligations with confidence.
