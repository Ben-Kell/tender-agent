# Defence-controlled waterfall delivery methodology

Fujitsu applies a structured Waterfall delivery methodology aligned to Defence requirements, ensuring all activities are planned, approved, executed, and evidenced under Defence control.

Each phase of delivery includes:
- Defined inputs and outputs
- Explicit Defence review and approval points
- Controlled progression between phases

Key phases include:
- Initiation
- Planning
- Execution
- Progress monitoring
- Quality assurance
- Change management
- Risk management
- Closure

Progression between phases only occurs following Defence approval, ensuring:
- No assumptions are made
- Risks are controlled
- Delivery remains aligned with Defence direction

All activities are recorded and reported through Defence-approved systems, ensuring full traceability from initiation through to completion.

This methodology supports development, enhancement, remediation, and transition activities within a controlled and auditable framework.
