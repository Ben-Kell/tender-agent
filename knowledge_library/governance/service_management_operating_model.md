# Defence-aligned service management governance model

Fujitsu’s service management model is designed to provide Defence with full visibility, control, and assurance over service delivery activities.

The model defines:
- How work is prioritised and executed
- How decisions are made and escalated
- How performance is monitored and reported
- How compliance is evidenced

All service activities are:
- Recorded in Defence-approved systems
- Managed through defined governance processes
- Reported through established governance forums

This ensures:
- Full traceability of service delivery
- Alignment with Defence operational requirements
- Continuous visibility of performance and outcomes

The model integrates incident management, change management, problem management, reporting, and continuous improvement into a single controlled framework.

This approach ensures service delivery is consistent, auditable, and aligned to Defence governance expectations.
