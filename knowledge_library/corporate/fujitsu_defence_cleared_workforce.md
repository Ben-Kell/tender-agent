# Fujitsu Defence cleared workforce and national scale capability

Fujitsu is a leading ICT service provider with over 50 years of experience delivering solutions across government, Defence, and commercial sectors in Australia and Oceania.

Fujitsu maintains a national workforce of over 3,500 personnel, including more than 1,100 security-cleared staff dedicated to Defence delivery. This cleared workforce enables Fujitsu to support services across all Australian states and territories and across multiple security domains, including Protected and above.

This geographic and workforce scale allows Fujitsu to support distributed and complex Defence environments, including enterprise and deployed operational contexts.

Fujitsu’s long-standing partnership with Defence spans multiple decades, during which it has delivered systems integration, ICT sustainment, application services, and operational support across both enterprise and deployed environments.

Through this capability, Fujitsu provides Defence with a scalable, secure, and nationally distributed delivery model that aligns with operational requirements and mission outcomes.
