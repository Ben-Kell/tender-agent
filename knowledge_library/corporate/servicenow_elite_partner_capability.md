# ServiceNow elite partner capability and platform expertise

Fujitsu, including its Enable division, is one of the largest ServiceNow Elite partners in the Asia-Pacific region, with a highly certified workforce capable of delivering large-scale ITSM and Enterprise Service Management transformations.

The Fujitsu ServiceNow capability includes:
- Over 350 consultants across all role types
- More than 1,000 certifications across the platform
- Expertise spanning all ServiceNow product suites, including Micro Certifications through to Master Architect level

This capability enables Fujitsu to deliver:
- Large-scale transformation programs
- Platform integration and optimisation
- Enterprise-wide service management solutions

Fujitsu continues to invest in expanding its ServiceNow capability to ensure access to current skills and emerging platform capabilities.

This depth of capability ensures Defence has access to specialist expertise, scalable delivery capacity, and proven transformation experience aligned to complex enterprise environments.
