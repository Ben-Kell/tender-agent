## Background

The [Commonwealth Agency] required a strategic transformation of its network and data services capability to improve reliability, security posture, and alignment with the Whole of Australian Government (WoAG) network architecture. Aging infrastructure, increasing cyber threats, and a move toward hybrid-cloud operations demanded a modernised, PROTECTED-level network environment.

## Our Approach

We delivered a fully managed network and data services solution that included:

- **Network Design & Refresh** – Replaced end-of-life core and edge network equipment with next-generation hardware, providing redundant, high-availability connectivity across [X] sites nationally.
- **PROTECTED Cloud Connectivity** – Established compliant connectivity to the PROTECTED cloud environments (AWS Secret Region / Azure Government), enabling the Agency to migrate sensitive workloads while maintaining ISM alignment.
- **24×7 SOC Integration** – Integrated network monitoring into our Security Operations Centre, providing continuous threat detection and automated alerting.
- **ISM & PSPF Alignment** – Conducted a comprehensive security uplift to achieve full alignment with current ISM controls and PSPF requirements, including encryption of data in transit and at rest.
- **Knowledge Transfer** – Delivered a structured training programme for Agency ICT staff, building sovereign capability and reducing dependence on external support.

## Transition Methodology

The transition from the incumbent provider was completed without service disruption using our phased transition methodology:

1. Mobilisation and baseline documentation (4 weeks).
2. Parallel operation with the incumbent (6 weeks).
3. Phased cutover across sites, prioritised by criticality (8 weeks).
4. Hypercare stabilisation period (4 weeks).
