DECLARATIONS

The Supplier declares that this deed poll is for the benefit of the Commonwealth of Australia as represented by the Department of Defence ABN 68 706 814 312 (‘Commonwealth’).

This deed poll is provided in connection with the Request for Quote [INSERT RFQ NUMBER] (RFQ) issued by the Commonwealth and the Quotation (Quotation) submitted by the Supplier in response to the RFQ.  Terms defined in the RFQ will have the same meaning when used in this deed poll.

For the avoidance of doubt, the RFQ process includes any ODIA process conducted by the Commonwealth and a reference to a Quotation in this deed poll includes a reference to a Quotation, or part of a Quotation, submitted to the Commonwealth pursuant to any ODIA process.

To the extent applicable, each declaration, undertaking, acknowledgement and agreement of the Supplier described in this deed poll is restated by the Supplier at the time of any further submission to the Commonwealth by the Supplier of the Quotation, or part of the Quotation, pursuant to any ODIA process.

The Supplier submits its Quotation to provide the Services solicited by the RFQ at the prices tendered and, subject to the statement of non-compliance included as part of its Quotation, in accordance with the draft Work Order.

The Supplier acknowledges and agrees:

to the Commonwealth’s rights as set out in the RFQ and this deed poll, including the Commonwealth's rights to exclude the Quotation;

that the Quotation has been prepared in accordance with the RFQ and is accurate, complete and not misleading;

that the Commonwealth can utilise all relevant information about the Supplier’s performance on Commonwealth procurement activities;

that the Supplier has conducted and will conduct itself during the RFQ process in a manner that is at least consistent with:

the Commonwealth’s obligations to act in accordance with the applicable Commonwealth procurement framework, for example to ensure certainty of costs and value for money; and

the requirements set out in the ‘Promoting Confidence in Defence Procurement Processes’ section of the Defence publication Defence and the Private Sector - An Ethical Relationship;

that the Commonwealth can rely on the Quotation in accurately assessing compliance with the RFQ, risks and risk management options, and value for money in accordance with the RFQ;

that representations made in the Quotation, when incorporated in any resultant Work Order, will be fully complied with by the Supplier;

that the Supplier has relied entirely upon its own inquiries and inspection in preparing its Quotation;

that the Supplier has not relied on any representation, letter, document or arrangement, whether oral or in writing, or other conduct of the Commonwealth, as adding to or amending the RFQ, except for any addendum issued by the Commonwealth that expressly add to or amend the RFQ;

that the Supplier does not have any judicial decisions against it (including overseas jurisdictions but excluding decisions under appeal or instances where the period for appeal or payment/settlement has not expired) relating to unpaid employee entitlements where the entitlements remain unpaid;

that it does not engage Illegal Workers;

that Defence, as a Commonwealth agency, is subject to legislative and administrative accountability and transparency requirements of the Commonwealth, including disclosures to Ministers and other Government representatives, Parliament and its Committees and the publication of information in respect of the RFQ process on the successful Supplier and information on any resultant Work Order on the AusTender website; and

that the Supplier is aware of the impact of the Auditor-General Act 1997 (Cth) on its participation in the RFQ and on any resultant Work Order and any Subcontract under a resultant Work Order.

The Supplier acknowledges and agrees that:

the RFQ and any communication or dealings of any kind in relation to the RFQ (other than this deed poll) between the Commonwealth and the Supplier, or between the Commonwealth and any other person with an interest in the RFQ, do not constitute a contract between the Commonwealth and the Supplier;

to the extent permitted by law, no binding contract (including a process contract) or other understanding on any basis whatsoever will exist between the Commonwealth and the Supplier unless and until a Work Order is signed by the Commonwealth and the Supplier; and

to the extent permitted by law, the Commonwealth has no liability to the Supplier, or any other person, for any compensation on any basis whatsoever in connection with the Supplier’s participation in the RFQ.

ACCEPTANCE

The Quotation submitted by the Supplier in response to the RFQ shall remain open for the Quotation Validity Period specified in the RFQ.

The Supplier acknowledges and agrees that the Quotation is an unconditional offer and, to the extent reasonably possible, the Supplier will obtain any necessary Authorisations to enable it to enter into any resultant Work Order on an unconditional basis.

the evaluation and clarification of Quotation; and

the conduct and content of negotiations, including final Work Order negotiations,
