# Corporate Overview

## About Us

We are a specialist technology and professional services company with a proven track record delivering mission-critical solutions to Australian Government and Defence clients. Established in Australia, we operate under Australian law, employ Australian citizens, and maintain the security clearances and compliance frameworks required to support sensitive government programs.

## Core Capabilities

- **Enterprise IT & Digital Transformation** – Design, integration, and management of complex enterprise technology environments.
- **Cyber Security & Information Assurance** – Protective monitoring, incident response, and alignment with the Australian Government Information Security Manual (ISM) and the Protective Security Policy Framework (PSPF).
- **Cloud & Infrastructure Services** – Secure cloud adoption, hybrid infrastructure, and managed services leveraging AWS, Azure, and on-premise platforms.
- **Data & Analytics** – Data engineering, business intelligence, and AI/ML solutions designed for government data-sovereignty requirements.
- **Programme & Project Management** – Delivery of large-scale programmes using PRINCE2, Agile, and hybrid methodologies certified by APMG and PMI.

## Defence Industry Security Program (DISP) Membership

We are a current member of the Defence Industry Security Program (DISP), demonstrating our commitment to protecting Australian national security interests. Our DISP membership level permits us to handle and store classified material up to the levels required for this engagement.
