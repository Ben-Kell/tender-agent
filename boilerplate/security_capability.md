# Security Capability

## Overview

Security is embedded across our entire service delivery model. We maintain a mature, risk-based approach to information and cyber security that is aligned with Australian Government frameworks and independently assessed.

## Information Handling

We handle government information in accordance with the Australian Government's classification framework:

- **OFFICIAL** and **OFFICIAL: Sensitive** information is managed under documented information-handling procedures.
- **PROTECTED** and above: handled only within appropriately accredited facilities and systems.
- Data is stored in Australia; no offshore transfer occurs without explicit written approval from the relevant authority.

## Cyber Security Operations

Our security operations capability includes:

- **Security Operations Centre (SOC):** 24×7 protective monitoring using SIEM and UEBA tooling.
- **Vulnerability Management:** Continuous scanning and patch management in line with ISM and ASD guidance.
- **Incident Response:** Documented IR plan tested annually; average time-to-contain [X] hours for critical incidents.
- **Penetration Testing:** Annual external and internal penetration testing conducted by CREST-certified providers.

## Physical Security

Our facilities meet the physical security requirements for the handling and storage of information up to [classification level]:

- Site security assessed in accordance with PSPF Zone model.
- CCTV, access control, and intruder detection systems are installed and maintained.
- Visitor management procedures are enforced at all sites.

## Supply Chain Security

We apply security requirements to our supply chain through contractual obligations, supplier assessments, and ongoing monitoring to mitigate third-party risk.
